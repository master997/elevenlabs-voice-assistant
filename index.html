Case study (short)

SDRs don’t need “more data.” They need the right 2–3 facts at the right moment, without tab switching.

What it does
  • Reads today’s meetings
  • Briefs accounts conversationally (not field-by-field)
  • Detects cross-call patterns (e.g., “Gemini”)
  • Drafts a Slack DM you can send to the team
Try these prompts
  • Start my morning
  • Notion first
  • Give me the top objection responses
  • Deutsche Telekom
  • Yes
